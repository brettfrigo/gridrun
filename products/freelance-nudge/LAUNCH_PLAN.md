# Launch Plan — Freelance Nudge

## Positioning
"Get paid faster without awkward follow-ups."

## Go-To-Market (Week 1)
1. Landing page + waitlist
2. 3 short demo videos (30s each)
3. Post in 10 freelancer communities
4. Cold outreach to 50 freelancers with overdue-invoice pain
5. Offer first 20 users a founder plan ($49/year)

## Revenue Targets
- Week 1: 5 paid users = ~$45 MRR
- Month 1: 25 paid users = ~$225 MRR
- Month 3: 100 paid users = ~$900 MRR

## Distribution Channels
- X/Twitter creator circles
- Indie Hackers
- Reddit (r/freelance, r/smallbusiness)
- Design/dev Slack communities

## Sales Script
"Quick one: I built a tiny app that helps freelancers send overdue invoice reminders in 30 seconds. If you’ve ever had to chase late payments, want early access?"
